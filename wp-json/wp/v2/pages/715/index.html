A<\/span>vTech Forum, India (ATFI) <\/span>works to facilitate favourable policy and legislative ecosystem required for the development of the aviation industry, while advancing the interests of its members. It looks to <\/span>create and sustain a suitable business environment by <\/span>working closely with members, regulatory authorities, policy makers<\/span> and other stakeholders. It <\/span>facilitates sharing of best practices, offers opportunities of networking and consensus building on key issues in addition to enhancing business opportunities.<\/span><\/p>

ATFI <\/span>is a <\/span>non-government, not-for-profit, and industry-led organization<\/span> w<\/span>ith an aim to\u00a0<\/span>promote growth, innovation, safety, economic growth and social development in the aviation sector.<\/span><\/p>

From influencing policy to encouraging debate, engaging with policy makers and civil society, ATFI articulates the views and concerns of industry segments it serves.<\/span><\/p>
